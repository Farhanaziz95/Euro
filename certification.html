International Organization for Standardization (ISO 9001)

ISO 9001 is defined as the international standard that specifies requirements for a quality managment system (QMS).
Organizations use the standard to demonstrate the ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and regulatory requirements.

OEKO-TEX

Oeko-Tex labels and certificates confirm the human-ecological safetu of textile products and leather articles from all stages of production (raw materials and fibres, yarns, fabrics, ready-to-use end products) along the textile value chain
STANDARD 100 is a product label for textile products from all processing stages that have been tested for harmful substances




Forest Stewardship Council(FSC)

Our License No:C169962

FSC (Forest Stewardship Council certification acts as a catalyst for improving standards in forest managment. it brings economic benefits and facilitates environmental benefits for biodiversity and eco systems
FSC certification ensures that products come from responsibly managed forests that provide environmental, social and economic benefits
