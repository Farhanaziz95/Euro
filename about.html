We Bring Design
Together With Technology

Ali-Brothers is a professional textile trim manufacturer and supplier that specializes in design, development, manufacture and supply of a wide variety of textile trims.

Our company is mainly engaged in producung medium and high-grade nylon lace, embroidered lace, cotton lace and other of garments and textile accessories including woven and printed labels, elastic bands and woven as well as satin ribbons

Our Mission

At Ali-Brothers we believe in building long term customer relationship, by providing high-quality affordable products with unmatched customer service.

Our mission is to build our brand loyalty by concentrating on quality instead of quantity. We want to expand our business and customer base while maintaining our trust bond through long-term relations which continue generation after genarations.

Our Vision

We strive to revive the textile industry by providing helping hand in the production and supply of exceptional quality raw material in the form of fine lace trims, labels and thread yarns

We also work hard in maintaining our quality standards by constant research and develoment in finding the innocatice products to fulfill our customer demands, in-keeping with the latest trends and technologies
